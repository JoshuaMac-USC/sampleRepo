Welcome to Linux

About Linux

Linux is a free and open-source operating system that is based on the Unix operating system. It was created by Linus Torvalds in 1991 and has since become one of the most popular and widely used operating systems in the world.

Why Choose Linux?

Linux is known for its stability, security, and flexibility. It's used in a wide range of applications, from servers and embedded systems to personal computers and smartphones. Linux offers a wealth of software and tools, and it's highly customizable to suit your needs.

Get Started with Linux

If you're interested in exploring Linux, you can start by downloading a Linux distribution such as Ubuntu, Fedora, or Debian. These distributions come with user-friendly installers and a wealth of software to get you started.
